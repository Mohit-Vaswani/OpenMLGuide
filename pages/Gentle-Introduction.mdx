# A Gentle Introdution

If you're new to the exciting world of ML, you're in the right place. This beginner-friendly section is your starting point on the journey to mastering the art and science of ML.

![Thinking AI](public/static/img/thinkingAI.png)

### Prerequisites
Before diving into ML, it's essential to have a strong foundation in Python programming. Python is the language of choice for ML practitioners, and it's a versatile, beginner-friendly language to learn. If you haven't already, we recommend starting with the [Python Data Science Handbook](https://jakevdp.github.io/PythonDataScienceHandbook/), an excellent book by Jake VanderPlas that covers Python essentials for data science and ML.

Once you're comfortable with Python, you'll be ready to explore the exciting world of ML. In this section, you'll find a curated collection of articles, books, and resources tailored to beginners. These materials will gently introduce the key ML concepts, algorithms, and techniques, providing a solid foundation for your journey into the realm of AI.

### Best Books to Get Started

- [The Hundred-page Machine Learning](https://github.com/severus27/ML-books/blob/main/The%20Hundred-Page%20Machine%20Learning%20Book/The%20Hundred-Page%20Machine%20Learning%20Book%20by%20Andriy%20Burkov.md): This book by Andriy Burkov offers an approachable explanation of machine learning fundamentals. Explore the world of ML, gaining insights into key concepts, practical applications, and potential R&D directions.

- Embark on your machine learning journey with [Machine Learning for Absolute Beginners](https://bmansoori.ir/book/Machine%20Learning%20For%20Absolute%20Beginners.pdf) by Oliver Theobald. This accessible book introduces newcomers to the fundamental principles of ML. Explore the ML landscape, acquire insights into essential concepts, and discover practical applications in a beginner-friendly manner.


### Courses

- [AI for Everyone](https://www.deeplearning.ai/courses/ai-for-everyone/): This course by  Deep Learning.AI offers a friendly introduction to AI's impact on various industries, empowering you to make informed decisions in today's AI-driven world. Explore the potential, grasp key concepts, and chart your course in the realm of AI innovation

- [Opportunities in AI](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5p248yoa3oE&t=105s): Andrew Ng's video on AI opportunities is a captivating exploration of the limitless potential within the AI landscape. Gain valuable insights into the transformative power of AI, discover real-world applications, and uncover pathways to harness AI for innovation and growth.

### Articles

- [AI in Healthcare](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC8285156/): Gain profound insights into how AI is revolutionizing the healthcare industry, from diagnosis to treatment, research, and patient care.

- [Robots in the Manufacturing Industry](https://www.wevolver.com/article/robots-in-the-manufacturing-industry-types-and-applications): Explore the dynamic landscape of automation, robotics, and smart technologies reshaping modern manufacturing. Gain insights into how robots are enhancing efficiency, precision, and innovation across production lines, and discover the evolving role of AI-driven automation in this transformative field.
