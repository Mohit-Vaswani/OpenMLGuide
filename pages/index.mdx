
# OpenMLGuide

Welcome to the world of Open Source Machine Learning! Our mission is to empower individuals to embark on an exciting journey into the realm of artificial intelligence and machine learning. This website is your gateway to a treasure trove of knowledge, insights, and resources sourced from some of the most reputable institutions and organizations in the field, including [Stanford Online](https://online.stanford.edu/), [DeepLearning.AI](https://www.deeplearning.ai/), [Fast.AI](https://www.fast.ai/), [3Blue1Brown](https://www.youtube.com/@3blue1brown) and many others.

![Local Image](public/static/img/geometricNeuralNetwork.jpg)

In the ever-evolving tech landscape, machine learning stands as a transformative force, unlocking new possibilities and reshaping global industries. Whether you're a seasoned data scientist, an enthusiastic learner, or entirely new to the world of ML, our platform caters to all expertise levels. Our goal is to democratize access to the vast universe of machine learning, making it approachable for everyone.

Here, you'll find a curated collection of free tutorials, guides, articles, courses, and projects, all designed to demystify complex concepts and equip you with the skills needed to excel in the world of ML.

Whether your interests lie in computer vision, natural language processing, reinforcement learning, deep learning or any other facet of machine learning, you'll discover a wealth of resources tailored to your needs. Our mission is not just to provide information but to foster a community of learners who can support and inspire each other on this exciting journey.


### How to navigate this website

- Browse Our Catalog: Explore our extensive library of ML resources. You'll find courses, lectures, tutorials, and more.

- Choose Your Path: Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, we have resources tailored to your needs. Select the courses that align with your goals and expertise level.

- Open Source, Open Access: All our content is open source and freely accessible. No paywalls, just knowledge waiting to be explored.

- Join Our Community: Connect with fellow learners and mentors on our [Discord]() server. Discuss concepts, ask questions, and collaborate on ML projects through our vibrant community.

### Contribute to Our Knowledge Base
 You can contribute by adding new resources, courses, posts or even enhancing existing content on our  [Github](https://github.com/jiraiyahiddenleaf/freeML-course) repository. Help us expand and improve the learning experience for everyone.
